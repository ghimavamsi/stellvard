About Dr. Sreedhar Chigurupati

Dr. Sreedhar Chigurupati leads Stellvard Bioscience, a deep-tech genomics enterprise delivering cutting-edge sequencing, bioinformatics, and life science solutions. His vision is to accelerate scientific discovery and precision healthcare through advanced genomics technologies.

Under his leadership, Stellvard has expanded its presence across India, the United Kingdom, GCC countries, and Africa while providing comprehensive genomics services for research, healthcare, agriculture, and biodiversity applications.
