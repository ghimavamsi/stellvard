Reed Biotech

High-Sensitivity ELISA Kits, Recombinant Proteins, and Customized Immunological Services.

About Reed Biotech

Reed Biotech is a premier biotechnology enterprise specializing in immunodiagnostic and research-grade reagents. With a focus on high-specificity ELISA kits, recombinant proteins, and custom antibody validation, Reed Biotech supports biomedical laboratories, clinical studies, and academic research globally through our official distribution network at Stellvard Bioscience.

Every product is subjected to stringent quality control workflows, guaranteeing high batch-to-batch consistency, low cross-reactivity, and exceptional detection sensitivity. This ensures research validation and reproducibility in critical therapeutic areas including oncology, immunology, neuroscience, and signal transduction pathways.

Featured Products & Solutions

We represent Reed Biotech’s comprehensive portfolio, delivering research products with reliable performance parameters:

High-Sensitivity ELISA Kits

Ready-to-use sandwich and competitive ELISA kits optimized for cytokine, chemokine, and hormone targets across multiple species (Human, Mouse, Rat, Porcine, etc.).

  • Human TNF-alpha ELISA Kit
  • Mouse IL-6 ELISA Kit
  • Rat IL-1 beta ELISA Kit

Recombinant Proteins

Highly pure recombinant proteins expressed in E. coli, Yeast, and Mammalian cell systems, verified for bioactivity and low endotoxin levels.

  • Recombinant Human IL-2 Protein
  • Recombinant Mouse TNF-alpha
  • Active Recombinant Human VEGF165

Primary & Secondary Antibodies

Monoclonal and polyclonal antibodies rigorously validated for applications such as Western Blotting (WB), Immunohistochemistry (IHC), and Flow Cytometry.

  • Rabbit Anti-GAPDH Polyclonal Antibody
  • Mouse Anti-Beta Actin Monoclonal Antibody
  • FITC / HRP Conjugated Secondary Antibodies

Customized ELISA Development

Specialized assay development, validation, and sample testing services tailored to pharmaceutical target discovery and preclinical research needs.

  • Target Antigen Expressing & Cloning
  • Antibody Pairing & Conjugation
  • Kit Optimization & Reproducibility Analysis

Scientific Impact & Capabilities

Reed Biotech's scientific capability guarantees that researchers get maximum return on their research investments. Through standardized processes, we deliver:

  • Strict QC Standards: Every batch is validated with internal controls and benchmark references.
  • High Sensitivity: Optimized antibody pairs enable detection at picogram levels.
  • Broad Coverage: Catalog support for over 500 clinical and research biomarkers.
