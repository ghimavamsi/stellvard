PacBio HiFi Sequencing for Precision Genomics

Stellvard Bioscience is a deep-tech genomics enterprise delivering advanced sequencing, bioinformatics, and life science solutions. With PacBio HiFi sequencing, Illumina platforms, Oxford Nanopore PromethION 24, and AI-powered genomic interpretation, Stellvard supports researchers, clinicians, and industry across India, GCC, and Africa.

The project focuses on end-to-end genomics including de novo sequencing, resequencing, transcriptomics, epigenomics, metagenomics, SNP genotyping, wildlife genomics, and custom bioinformatics workflows. It helps resolve complex genomic questions through high-accuracy long-read sequencing and structured data analysis.

Solutions & Capabilities

Stellvard provides comprehensive genomics solutions powered by PacBio HiFi, Oxford Nanopore PromethION 24, Illumina sequencing systems, and AI-powered bioinformatics. These capabilities support rare disease analysis, complex cancers, structural variants, telomere-to-telomere assemblies, and research-grade genomic interpretation.

From sample submission to sequencing, analysis, reporting, and research support, Stellvard offers a streamlined workflow for human genomics, plant and animal genomics, agricultural genomics, transcriptomics, metagenomics, epigenomics, and bioinformatics.

Stellvard’s genomics solutions enable researchers and clinicians to study rare diseases, complex cancers, microbial communities, agricultural traits, biodiversity, and population genetics with advanced sequencing and data interpretation support.
