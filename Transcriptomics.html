Transcriptomics Services

O ur transcriptomics services provide high-resolution profiling of gene expression, alternative splicing, and novel transcript discovery. By studying the complete set of RNA transcripts in a cell or tissue, we enable researchers to understand functional cellular responses under various environmental and disease conditions, accelerating biomarker and drug target discovery.

01

RNA Isolation & QC

High-yield isolation of total RNA from diverse sample types, followed by stringent quality control assessing purity and integrity scores.
02

Library Prep & Sequencing

Construction of stranded, ribosomal-depleted, or poly(A)-selected RNA libraries followed by deep next-generation sequencing.
03

Differential Expression

Advanced bioinformatics to quantify gene levels, discover alternative splicing, and profile pathway and network biology.

Gene Expression Dynamics & Biomarker Discovery

Transcriptomic sequencing provides a dynamic snapshot of gene expression patterns across different physiological conditions or treatment groups. It is widely used to identify differentially expressed genes, find novel splice variants, and detect fusion transcripts in cancer genomes. Our high-depth stranded RNA-seq workflows ensure precise quantification, supporting diagnostic biomarker discovery and functional genomics validation.
